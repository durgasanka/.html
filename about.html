About VIT-AP University, Amaravati

The university was established in the year 2017. VIT- AP University offers various courses at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels. For admission into the VIT- AP University candidates need to appear and clear the entrance examination VITEEE. The university offers placement opportunities to the students of which the highest CTC offered to the students is found to be Rs. 9,23,000. Apart from these the university also provides various types of campus facilities to the students as well as the staff members working in the college.

VIT AP University Courses

The university offers various types of courses to students in different fields like engineering, law, management and many more. The duration of the courses ranges from 2 years to 6 years. VIT AP University BBA course fee is present in three different categories for category-1 Rs. 58,000, category-2 Rs. 78,000 and for the category-3 it is found to be Rs. 1,03,000. The fee structure for the courses offered by VIT AP University ranges from Rs. 43,000 to Rs. 1,98,000.

VIT- AP University Admissions

The University Amravati admissions are offered at undergraduate, postgraduate, dual degree and doctoral levels. The university accepts scores of VITEEE for B.Tech programme admission. VITMEE for M.Tech VLSI programme admission. As per the VIT- AP University admission criteria, shortlisted candidates have to appear for counselling like VITEEE counselling for the B.Tech programme, university-level counselling for B.Com (Finance) programme and so on. Finally, selected candidates have to submit the documents and pay the fees.

VIT-AP University, Amaravati Placements

The university has a career development centre to provide placement assistance to final-year students. Placement training like soft skills, aptitude tests, resume building, coding tests, group discussions, and mock interviews are also provided to ensure the successful placement of the candidates. VIT- AP University Amravati placement is conducted on-campus. Some of the recruiters of the university are Intel, Ebay, Amazon, Dell, Hitachi, HP, Honda, Ford, TCS, Accenture, Wipro, and Infosys.

VIT-AP University, Amaravati Location

The university is located at G-30, Inavolu, beside AP Secretariat Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. The nearest Railway Station is Vijayawada Junction Railway Station. It is 18.3 km far from the university and would take around 38 minutes. The nearest Bus Stop is Mangalagiri Bus Stop. It is located at a distance of 16.9 km and would take around 37 minutes. Vijayawada International Airport is the nearest airport. The distance between the airport and the university is 37.3 km and would take approximately 1 hour and 19 minutes to reach the campus. Students can hire a cab or an auto to reach the campus gate.
